LineClear — the wired open-ear headset we wish existed.

Avantree-style earhooks feel great all day, but inline mics fall apart in loud venues. LineClear fixes that with a detachable call mic module that’s discreet, rugged, and routed down your back/shirt — no Bluetooth required.

Goal: Take startup calls while pushing cases on a noisy stage — without callers hearing the whole room.
Open-ear stays open. The mic does the heavy lifting.

What we’re building

Two modes: Listen (no mic attached) and Call (detachable directional mic).

Why detachable?

A fixed boom is effective but not always welcome. Detachable lets you stay low-profile until you need pro call clarity.

The current market is a triangle you can’t win

  • Open-ear + wired Comfort + awareness, but calls pick up everything.
  • Noise-proof calls Usually requires a boom mic or wireless DSP.
  • Discreet all-day wear Inline mics are too far from your mouth in loud environments.

Real stagehand requirements

Designed for load-ins, comms moments, and long shifts.

  • No ear-canal sealLess fatigue, more awareness.
  • Cable stays controlledBehind neck, down back, clipped.
  • Mic must reject noiseDirectional + closer to mouth.
  • Works on phonesUSB-C with proper DAC.

The solution: split “listening” from “calling”

Keep the open-ear comfort you already like — then add a detachable mic when you need it. This is a hardware-first solution that doesn’t rely on Bluetooth radios or heavy noise algorithms.

PHONE (USB-C) │ ├── USB-C DAC inline pod (volume/mute) │ ├── main cable up inside shirt │ ├── Y-split behind neck │ ├─ Left open-ear hook speaker │ └─ Right open-ear hook speaker │ └── (optional) Detachable mic module ├─ clips at collar / chest strap └─ points toward mouth (directional)

Modes

  • Listen Mode No mic attached. Clean, low-profile, comfortable all day.
  • Call Mode Attach mic module near collar. Better voice focus in noise.
Key idea: Put the mic where it works (near your mouth), while keeping the headset where it’s comfortable (open-ear).

Detachable mic module (what it should be)

  • Directional (cardioid-ish)Rejects room wash better than inline mics.
  • Clips like a lavCollar, shirt seam, chest strap, harness.
  • Quick attachMagnetic or locking micro connector.
  • Windscreen includedUsable outdoors and on docks/ramps.

Open-ear earhooks (Avantree vibe, but rugged)

  • Soft hook geometryStable during movement; helmet-friendly.
  • Speech-tuned EQCalls/podcasts stay intelligible.
  • Replaceable cable sectionsField repair beats re-buying.
  • Matte, low-glare finishDiscreet on show site.
